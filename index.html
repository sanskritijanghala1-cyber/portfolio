About Me

hi! my name is sanskriti janghala. i'm an economics student at symbiosis school of economics, navigating the intersection of analytical logic and visual storytelling.

i've been creative since i was a child. while others were looking inside or outside the box, i found myself fascinated by the lines of the box. i loved that space because it allowed me to be infinitely creative while respecting real-world constraints, a perspective i carry into my design work today.

my journey has always involved art in some form, whether through the rhythm of poems, the precision of drawings, or the energy of video editing. all those paths have led me here, where i combine my background in economics with a lifelong passion for creating things that matter.
